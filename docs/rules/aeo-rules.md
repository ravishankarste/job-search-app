# Workflow: Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

The internet is shifting from "10 blue links" to AI-generated answers. To succeed, Udyog Marg must be the trusted source that AI systems want to cite.

### 🏛️ The AEO Philosophy
- **Don't just rank—Become the answer.**
- **Clear Intent**: Every page must answer a specific question immediately.
- **AI-Friendly Structure**: Use Schema markup and clear semantic HTML so crawlers can easily summarize our value.

### 🛠️ Step 1: Direct Answer Pattern
- Place the most important information (e.g., "What is Udyog Marg?") in the **top 20%** of the page.
- Use an "Inverted Pyramid" style: Conclusion first, details second.

### 🛠️ Step 2: Structured Content
- **FAQ Style**: Use `<h2>` or `<h3>` as questions and the following paragraph as the concise answer.
- **Data over Fluff**: Use original insights, statistics, and data points that AI models love to cite.
- **Lists over Walls**: Use bullet points and tables (like we do in the Dashboard) for high readability.

### 🛠️ Step 3: Distribution & Authority
- **LinkedIn/Reddit Strategy**: Share insights, not just links.
- **Brand Mentions**: The more Udyog Marg is mentioned in trusted communities, the more "Authority" AI systems will give us.
- **Social Proof**: Genuine user feedback (from our Feedback Scribe) acts as a high-trust signal for Answer Engines.

### 🚀 Implementation Checklist
- [ ] Direct answer near the top?
- [ ] Clear, question-based headings?
- [ ] Structured data (JSON-LD) where applicable?
- [ ] Concise, useful information with zero fluff?
