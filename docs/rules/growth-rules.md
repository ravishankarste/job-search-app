# Workflow: Growth and Outreach

Follow these steps when promoting Udyog Marg or talking to potential users.

### Step 1: Use the Right Message
Test these three "Hooks" to see which one people like best:
- "Stop guessing. See your ATS match score before you apply."
- "The Job Search OS for people who hate applying to jobs."
- "10x your interview rate by bridging the gap between your resume and the job description."

### Step 2: Value-First Outreach
When messaging a job seeker on LinkedIn or Twitter:
- Don't just say "Try my tool."
- Say: "I saw you're looking for [Role]. I noticed that roles like that usually want [Skill X]. My tool shows a 90% match score if you add that. Want the full report?"

### Step 3: The "Loom" Power Move
- For high-value users, record a 60-second video of you analyzing their target job using Udyog Marg. 
- Send it to them to prove the tool works for their specific needs.

### Step 4: Capture Success
- Every time someone says "I got an interview using this!", save the message.
- Ask them if we can share their "Before and After" match scores.
