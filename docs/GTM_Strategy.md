# 🚀 Udyog Marg: Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

This document outlines the strategic roadmap for launching Udyog Marg and acquiring the first 100 high-value customers.

## 🎯 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
*Don't target everyone. Target the person who feels the most pain.*

### The "Frustrated High-Performer"
- **Role**: Software Engineers, Product Managers, or Data Scientists.
- **Experience**: 3-8 years (Mid-to-Senior).
- **Pain Point**: They have great skills but are stuck in the "LinkedIn Black Hole"—applying to dozens of jobs and getting automated rejections because they haven't optimized for the ATS.
- **Urgency**: Active job seekers or those "quietly looking" for a better role in a competitive market.

---

## 💬 2. The Core Message (The "Hook")
*Testing the resonance of our copy.*

- **Draft A (Performance)**: "Stop guessing. See your ATS match score before you apply."
- **Draft B (Efficiency)**: "The Job Search OS for people who hate applying to jobs."
- **Draft C (Growth)**: "10x your interview rate by bridging the gap between your resume and the job description."

**Action**: Run these three headlines as subject lines in focused outreach to see which gets the highest open/reply rate.

---

## 📧 3. Focused Outreach (Early Adopters)

### 3.1 Social Engagement
- **Strategy**: Don't just post "I built this." Engage with people posting about their job search struggles on LinkedIn and Twitter.
- **The Value-Add**: "I saw you're struggling with [specific job]. I ran the description through my tool and it looks like [Skill X] is the missing piece. Happy to share the full analysis if you want?"

### 3.2 Value-First Email
- **Template**:
  > "Hi [Name], I saw you're looking for [Role]. I noticed that [Role] usually prioritizes [Specific Skill]. I built a tool that checks your resume against that. I ran a quick check for you—here's one thing you could tweak to hit a 90% match score."

### 3.3 The "Loom" Power Move
- **Action**: Record a 60-second video of yourself using Udyog Marg to analyze a specific job the prospect is interested in. 
- **Effect**: It proves the product works and shows you've done the work specifically for them.

---

## 🛠 4. Landing Page as a Customer Tool
*The landing page isn't just an ad; it's the first step of the product.*

- **The Interactive Hook**: Allow users to paste a Job URL directly on the landing page to get a "Preview Match Score."
- **Feedback Loop**: Share mockups of new features (like the AI Interview Coach) with your early email list *before* building them. Ask: "Would this save you an hour of prep time?"

---

## 📈 5. Beyond the First 100 Customers

### 5.1 Success Story Library
- Capture every "I got an interview!" message. 
- Create a dedicated "Hall of Fame" on the site showing the before/after match scores of successful candidates.

### 5.2 Referral Program (The "Viral Loop")
- **Mechanism**: "Invite a friend to optimize their resume and you both get [Premium Feature/Credit]."
- **Focus**: Targeted at peer-to-peer sharing among colleagues.

### 5.3 Thought Leadership
- Publish data-driven articles: *"We analyzed 1,000 LinkedIn jobs—here are the top 5 skills recruiters actually care about in 2026."*
