# 🚀 Udyog Marg: Your 5-Minute Career Test Drive

**Step 0: Join the Path (Sign Up)** 🔐

1. **Visit**: [upanita.com/jobs](https://upanita.com/jobs)
2. **Create Account**: Click on **"Join Alpha"** (or "Sign Up").
3. **Go**: Enter your email and password to enter the dashboard immediately.

---

**Step 1: The "Pipeline" (Add a Job)** 📝

1. **Job Pipeline**: Go to the **Job Pipeline** tab and click the **"+"** button.
2. **Fill Details**: Manually enter a Job Title (e.g., "Senior Frontend Engineer") and a Company (e.g., "Google").
3. **Save**: Click **Save to Pipeline**.

---

**Step 2: The "Brain" (Match Score)** 🧠

1. **Upload Resume**: Go to the **Resumes** tab and upload your PDF resume.
2. **Link It**: Open the job you created, and in the sidebar, click your resume to **Link** it.
3. **Analyze**: Check your **ATS Match Score** to see your % and Skills Gap analysis!

---

**Step 3: The "Coach" (Interview Prep)** 🎙️

1. **Launch Prep**: On the same Job Detail page, click **"Generate Prep Guide"** in the Interview Prep Mode widget.
2. **Review**: Check your **Elevator Pitch** and the **"Trap" Question** strategy.

---

**Need Help?** 🤖
Click **Margi** (the pulsing orange button in the bottom-right) to chat!

---

**Happy Hunting!** 🎯🔥
