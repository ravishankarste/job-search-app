# 🚀 Udyog Marg: User Testing Journey Guide

Welcome! This guide is designed to help you experience the full power of the **Job Search OS** in under 10 minutes. Follow these three "Golden Journeys" to see the magic happen.

---

## 🗺️ Journey 1: The "Smart Discovery" (3 mins)
*Goal: See how the app automates the boring part of job hunting.*

1.  **Find a Job**: Go to LinkedIn or Indeed and find a job you like (e.g., a "React Developer" role).
2.  **Paste the Link**: In the **Discovery** box on the Udyog Marg dashboard, paste the URL.
3.  **The Magic**: Watch the 15-second "Discovery Sprint."
    *   *Success Check*: Did the app open a modal with the Company Name and Job Title already filled in?
4.  **Save**: Hit "Save Job" to move it to your pipeline.

---

## 🧠 Journey 2: The "Resume Intelligence" (4 mins)
*Goal: See how the app 'reads' your background and calculates fit.*

1.  **Upload**: Go to the **Resumes** tab and upload a PDF of your resume.
2.  **Link It**: Go back to your **Job Pipeline**, open the job you just saved, and in the sidebar, click your resume to **Link** it.
3.  **The Analysis**: Look at the **ATS Match Score** box.
    *   *Success Check*: Do you see your Match %? Do you see which skills you have (Green) and which ones you're missing (Orange)?
4.  **Draft**: Click the **"Magic Draft (AI)"** button in the Cover Letter section.
    *   *Success Check*: Did it write a professional letter that mentions your specific skills?

---

## 🎙️ Journey 3: The "Interview Prep" (3 mins)
*Goal: Get ready for the big day with your virtual coach.*

1.  **Generate**: On the same Job Detail page, find the **Interview Prep Mode** widget.
2.  **Coach Mode**: Click **"Generate Prep Guide."**
3.  **The Strategy**:
    *   *Success Check*: Look at the **"Trap" Question** (the one about a skill you're missing). Does the "Pro-Tip" help you handle it?
    *   *Success Check*: Read the **Elevator Pitch**. Does it sound like you?

---

## 🛠️ Need Help?
If you see **"N/A"** or an error, it usually means the job link is behind a security wall or the PDF text is unreadable. Let the developer know!

**Happy Hunting!** 🎯🔥
